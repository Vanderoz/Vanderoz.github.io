Dokdo

The most chillest an relaxing game if you want to remove your stress then this game is for you. it's concept is so simple its about conquering island with your boat. but  to conquer them all you will need to upgrade your boat and to upgrade your boat you will need to farm fishes or other npc.

Time flies when playing this game you will enjoy your time playing this game. it's for people who have spare time but does not want to play any competitive game and its for people that is new to the world of mobile games.
